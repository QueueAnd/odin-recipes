Bologna Cake

Description

If you need a unique appetizer for a retro-themed party, this bologna cake recipe is for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sliced bologna
  • 2 tablespoons grated onion
  • 1 (8-ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 package Ritz crackers, for serving

Steps

  1. Beat together softened cream cheese, grated onion, and Worcestershire sauce at medium speed until smooth and fluffy.
  2. Assemble the cake: spread a small amount of cream cheese in the center of a serving plate. Put a slice of bologna on top, then spread more cream cheese mixture on top. Repeat until all the bologna slices are used.
  3. Frost the cake with the remaining cream cheese mixture.
  4. Refrigerate to set. Slice and serve with crackers. Enjoy!
